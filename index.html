episode 03: Ava Peplinski

By Clayton Gerrans / April 30th 2025 / 20:04

In this episode, Clayton spoke to Ava Peplinski, an undergraduate student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Unlike most students, Ava spends her Fridays volunteering for the Community Immigration Law Center. Here, she assists Spanish-speaking clients with organizing general information on their immigration status, often in hopes of helping her local Madison migrant community members seek asylum.
